Quality work, crafted by careful hands.

Butte Builders Inc. specializes in residential and commercial interior finish carpentry for homeowners and contractors. It is owned and operated by Eric Butte, who has over 10 years of experience in the carpentry trade. Butte Builders does everything from small carpentry repairs to large commercial installs. Eric is committed to quality work and customer satisfaction. He conducts every estimate and is present on the job site, making sure it is completed professionally and meets, or even exceeds, the customers expectations.

Examples of some of the work Butte Builders can do or install for you are:

- prehung doors

- window jambs and casing

- rails

- hardwood treads and risers

- wainscoting

- cabinets

and so much more!

Bringing your vision to life is what Butte Builders does!

Butte Builders appreciates the relationships they have with its customers and strives to meet their every need. If you are looking for quality craftsmanship, at a great value, you have come to the right place. We look forward to working with you!

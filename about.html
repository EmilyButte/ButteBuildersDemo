Quality work, crafted by careful hands.

Hi My name is Eric Butte and I am the founder of Butte Builders Inc. I do all the estimates and am on every new job making sure its installed professionally and to the standards that were discussed and agreed on. We specialize in residential and commercial interior finish carpentry for homeowners and contractors. I have been in the carpentry trade for over 11 years and have done jobs as small as trim repairs to as large as commercial cabinet installs and anything in between.

Butte Builders installs prehung doors, window jambs and casing, rails, hardwood treads and risers, wainscoting, cabinets, and so much more. Bringing your vision to life is what we do!

We appreciate the relationships we have with our customers and strive to meet your every need. If you are looking for quality craftsmanship at a great value you have come to the right place. I look forward to working with you!

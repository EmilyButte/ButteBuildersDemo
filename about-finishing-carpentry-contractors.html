Who We Are

Butte Builders Inc. specializes in residential and commercial interior finish carpentry for homeowners and contractors. It is owned and operated by Eric Butte, who has over 10 years of experience in the carpentry trade. Butte Builders does everything from small carpentry repairs to large commercial installs.

Finish Carpentry Is What We Do

Eric is committed to quality work and customer satisfaction. He conducts every estimate and is present on the job site, making sure it is completed professionally and meets, or even exceeds, the customer's expectations.

Examples of some of the work Butte Builders can do for you are:

  • installation of prehung doors
  • window jambs and casing
  • rails
  • hardwood treads and risers
  • wainscoting
  • cabinets
  • and so much more!

Bringing your vision to life is what Butte Builders does!

Our Commitment to Customer Satisfaction

Butte Builders appreciates the relationships they have with its customers and strives to meet their every need. If you are looking for quality craftsmanship, at a great value, you have come to the right place. We look forward to working with you!
